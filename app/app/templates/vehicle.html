Terms & Conditions

  • 01
    Mandatory Third-Party Cover

    At minimum, third-party liability cover is compulsory under the Motor Vehicles Act. Driving without it is a punishable offence.

  • 02
    Depreciation Deduction

    Standard policies apply depreciation on parts during claims. A zero-depreciation (nil dep) add-on eliminates this deduction on claim settlements.

  • 03
    Insured Declared Value (IDV)

    IDV is the maximum claim amount — it equals current market value minus depreciation. A higher IDV means higher premium but better compensation.

  • 04
    Policy Exclusions

    Damage due to drunk driving, driving without a valid licence, or use for commercial purposes outside policy scope are standard exclusions.

  • 05
    FIR Requirement

    For theft or major accidents, an FIR must be filed with police and a copy submitted to the insurer within 24–48 hours for claim processing.

Commonly Offered Benefits

What You Get

  • Own Damage Cover

    Full comprehensive cover includes damage to your own vehicle due to accidents, fire, floods, natural disasters, and vandalism.

  • No Claim Bonus (NCB)

    Earn 20–50% discount on Own Damage premium for each claim-free year — transferable even when switching insurers.

  • Zero Depreciation Add-on

    Ensures 100% of repair/replacement cost is covered without age-based depreciation cuts — highly recommended for new vehicles.

  • Roadside Assistance (RSA)

    24x7 on-road help for breakdowns, flat tyres, fuel delivery, towing, and emergency key service — usually free or low-cost add-on.

  • Engine & Gearbox Protect

    Optional add-on covers engine and gearbox damage due to water ingression or lubricant leakage — standard policies don't include this.

Before You Buy

Smart Tips

💡

Always opt for zero-depreciation add-on for vehicles less than 5 years old — it pays for itself in one claim.

💡

Don't file small claims — preserve your NCB. The 20–50% discount on renewal saves far more than small repairs.

💡

Choose a plan with a wide cashless garage network in your city for hassle-free repairs without upfront payments.

💡

Set IDV at correct market value — don't lower it just to save on premium; underinsurance leads to lower claim payouts.

💡

Install IRDAI-approved anti-theft devices like Gear Lock / GPS — it gets you a 2.5–5% discount on Own Damage premium.
